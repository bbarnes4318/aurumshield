AURUMSHIELD NETWORK PARTICIPANT AGREEMENT

AurumShield Institutional Settlement Network

Operator: PVN LLC
Jurisdiction: Delaware / New York

Effective Date: [DATE]

1. INTRODUCTION

This Network Participant Agreement (“Agreement”) establishes the rights and obligations governing participation in the AurumShield Institutional Settlement Network (“Network”).

The Network is a proprietary settlement infrastructure operated by PVN LLC, enabling institutional participants to settle transactions involving allocated physical bullion through deterministic Delivery-versus-Payment (DvP) mechanisms.

Participation in the Network is restricted to qualified institutional entities.

2. DEFINITIONS

Allocated Bullion
Physical precious metals stored in segregated vault custody and allocated to a participant.

AurumShield Ledger
The internal settlement ledger operated by PVN LLC which records digital title to allocated bullion.

DvP Engine
The deterministic settlement system responsible for executing simultaneous transfer of bullion title and settlement value.

Participant
An entity approved by AurumShield to access the network.

Settlement Asset
A financial instrument used for settlement, including fiat funds received via Fedwire or digital settlement assets.

3. PARTICIPANT ELIGIBILITY

Entities eligible to participate in the Network include:

• corporations
• hedge funds
• banks
• broker-dealers
• commodity trading firms
• family offices

Retail participants are not permitted.

Each participant must demonstrate sufficient financial capacity and operational sophistication.

4. PARTICIPANT TIERS

Participants may be assigned to one of the following tiers:

Tier 1 — Liquidity Providers

Entities authorized to provide bid and offer pricing and liquidity to the network.

Tier 2 — Settlement Participants

Institutional entities executing bullion settlement transactions.

Tier 3 — Treasury Participants

Corporate treasuries utilizing the network for settlement of commercial obligations.

AurumShield may modify participant tiers as necessary.

5. PARTICIPANT ONBOARDING

Before accessing the Network, each participant must complete:

• institutional KYC/KYB verification
• execution of the Master Commercial Agreement
• execution of this Participant Agreement
• acceptance of risk disclosures
• configuration of settlement instructions

6. CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS

Participants must maintain sufficient capital to support settlement obligations.

Minimum capital requirements may include:

• net asset thresholds
• collateral requirements
• liquidity commitments

AurumShield may adjust capital requirements based on market conditions.

7. COLLATERAL REQUIREMENTS

Participants must maintain collateral sufficient to cover outstanding settlement obligations.

Collateral may include:

• fiat balances
• allocated bullion balances

AurumShield maintains a first-priority security interest in collateral held within participant accounts.

8. SETTLEMENT LIMITS

Participants may be subject to transaction limits based on:

• financial capacity
• liquidity conditions
• regulatory considerations

Limits may include:

• daily settlement limits
• per-transaction limits
• aggregate exposure limits

9. SETTLEMENT PROCESS

All transactions are processed through the AurumShield DvP settlement engine.

Settlement occurs only when both conditions are satisfied:

Payment value is confirmed

Bullion title is available

The system then executes simultaneous transfer.

10. SETTLEMENT FINALITY

Once a transaction is finalized by the DvP engine:

• ownership records are updated
• settlement value is transferred
• the transaction becomes irreversible

Participants acknowledge that settlement cannot be reversed once finalized.

11. USE OF DIGITAL SETTLEMENT ASSETS

The Network may utilize digital settlement assets such as USDC or USDT as temporary settlement rails.

Such assets are used solely for operational settlement purposes.

AurumShield does not issue digital assets.

12. VAULT CUSTODY

Bullion purchased through the Network is stored with institutional vault providers including:

Malca-Amit Global Vaulting Services

Bullion is stored on an allocated basis.

Participants retain legal ownership of allocated bullion.

13. PHYSICAL DELIVERY

Participants may request physical delivery of bullion subject to:

• vault processing procedures
• transportation arrangements
• minimum bar sizes

Transportation costs are borne by the participant.

14. LIQUIDITY PROVIDERS

Liquidity Providers supply executable market prices for bullion.

Liquidity Providers must:

• maintain sufficient trading capacity
• honor executed transactions
• settle transactions within agreed timeframes

Failure to settle may result in removal from the Network.

15. DEFAULT MANAGEMENT

If a participant fails to meet settlement obligations, AurumShield may:

• suspend transactions
• liquidate collateral
• terminate network access

Collateral liquidation may occur through approved liquidity providers.

16. MARKET DISRUPTION PROCEDURES

In extraordinary market conditions AurumShield may implement emergency measures including:

• temporary settlement suspension
• liquidity provider substitution
• system maintenance procedures

These actions are taken to preserve market stability.

17. NETWORK SECURITY

Participants must maintain appropriate cybersecurity safeguards.

Unauthorized access must be reported immediately.

Participants are responsible for safeguarding authentication credentials.

18. CONFIDENTIALITY

Participants must maintain confidentiality regarding:

• proprietary network systems
• participant identities
• transaction information

Disclosure is permitted only where required by law.

19. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

Participants must comply with applicable laws including:

• anti-money laundering regulations
• sanctions compliance
• commodities regulations

AurumShield may suspend accounts suspected of regulatory violations.

20. AMENDMENT OF NETWORK RULES

AurumShield may modify Network rules to maintain system integrity.

Participants will receive notice of material changes.

Continued participation constitutes acceptance of updated rules.

21. TERMINATION

AurumShield may terminate participation if a participant:

• violates network rules
• fails to meet settlement obligations
• poses a risk to network stability

Participants may terminate participation with written notice.

22. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

To the maximum extent permitted by law, AurumShield shall not be liable for losses arising from:

• market fluctuations
• banking system failures
• blockchain network disruptions

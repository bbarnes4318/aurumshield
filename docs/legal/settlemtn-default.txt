AURUMSHIELD SETTLEMENT DEFAULT MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

Network: AurumShield Institutional Settlement Network
Operator: PVN LLC

1. PURPOSE

This Settlement Default Management Framework establishes procedures for addressing participant failures to meet settlement obligations.

The framework ensures orderly resolution of failed settlements while protecting the integrity of the AurumShield Network.

2. DEFAULT EVENTS

A participant is considered in default if it fails to:

• deliver required settlement funds
• deliver required bullion balances
• meet collateral requirements
• comply with regulatory obligations

3. DEFAULT DETECTION

The AurumShield settlement engine continuously monitors participant accounts.

If a participant lacks sufficient funds or collateral to complete settlement, the system flags the account.

4. DEFAULT RESPONSE PROCEDURE

Upon detection of a default event, AurumShield may implement the following actions:

Step 1 — Transaction Suspension

Pending settlement transactions involving the defaulting participant are suspended.

Step 2 — Collateral Evaluation

AurumShield evaluates collateral held in the participant’s account.

Collateral may include:

• fiat balances
• allocated bullion

Step 3 — Collateral Liquidation

If necessary, AurumShield may liquidate collateral to cover outstanding settlement obligations.

Liquidation may occur through approved liquidity providers.

Step 4 — Network Protection

The defaulting participant may be temporarily or permanently removed from the network.

5. LOSS ALLOCATION

Losses resulting from a default are covered in the following order:

participant collateral

liquidation proceeds

recovery from the participant

AurumShield does not operate a mutualized loss pool.

Participants do not share losses from other participants’ defaults.

6. EMERGENCY MARKET CONDITIONS

In extraordinary market conditions, AurumShield may implement temporary measures including:

• settlement delays
• liquidity provider substitution
• trading suspensions

These measures are intended to maintain orderly market functioning.

7. NETWORK STABILITY

AurumShield reserves the right to take any action reasonably necessary to preserve system integrity.

Such actions may include:

• restricting participant activity
• adjusting collateral requirements
• suspending network operations.

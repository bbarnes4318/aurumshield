Purpose

This document evaluates the inherent money laundering risk associated with AurumShield’s business model and identifies the controls implemented to mitigate those risks.

1. Risk Assessment Methodology

AurumShield applies a three-stage risk scoring model.

Stage 1 — Identify Inherent Risk

Assess risk present before controls.

Stage 2 — Evaluate Control Strength

Evaluate the strength of the compliance controls.

Stage 3 — Determine Residual Risk

Residual risk = inherent risk minus control effectiveness.

2. Risk Scoring Scale
Score	Risk Level
1	Low
2	Moderate
3	Elevated
4	High
5	Severe

Residual risk is calculated using:

Residual Risk = Inherent Risk – Control Strength

10. Risk Mitigation Strategy

AurumShield mitigates AML risk through:

• automated identity verification
• sanctions screening
• transaction monitoring
• deterministic settlement controls

The system operates under a Fail-Closed Compliance Model, meaning:

Transactions cannot execute unless compliance clearance is achieved.

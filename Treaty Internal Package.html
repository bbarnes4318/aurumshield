Concern: Startup risk, no empirical triangles.

Correct. This is not legacy insurance. Loss modeling is structural: Deterministic claim triggers, hardstop exposure caps, and binary adjudication. We do not rely on long-tail actuarial development patterns.

Concern: Participants could engineer failure to trigger indemnity.

Claims require cryptographic state validation and third-party confirmation. Deductibles increase with TRI band. No counterparty profits from an engineered breach.

Objection 6: "Who controls hub risk?"

Concern: Hub compromise is catastrophic.

Max 35% single-hub concentration is enforced. Phase 2 includes a multi-hub diversification mandate. Hub risk is modeled as a primary tail driver.

AurumShield — Institutional Demo Walkthrough

Audience: Investor, partner, or executive being presented a guided demonstration.

Live URL: https://aurumshield.vip

Duration: 20–30 minutes.

Prerequisites

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox).
  • Internet connection to aurumshield.vip.
  • No login credentials needed — uses one-click role selection.

1. Entering Demo Mode

Step 1.1 — Navigate to demo login

https://aurumshield.vip/demo/login?demo=true

Important: The ?demo=true parameter is required to activate the environment.

Step 1.2 — Select "Clearing Authority"

Click the card labeled "Clearing Authority". This role provides full visibility across all modules.

System Action: Seeds a deterministic scenario (listing, reservation, order, settlement) and redirects to Dashboard.

2. Dashboard Overview

Section Talking Point
Capital Summary "All capital metrics are derived in real time from actual settlement state — not static mock numbers."
Settlement Queue "Every settlement in the pipeline is tracked with deterministic status transitions."
Breach Card "Breach events are generated deterministically when capital thresholds are exceeded."

3. Marketplace & Reservation Flow

Step 3.1 — View

Navigate to Marketplace. Show the listing details: Form, Purity, Weight, Vault Location.

Step 3.2 — Reserve

Click Reserve. Enter weight. Show the pre-trade policy check (TRI Score, ECR Impact).

Talking Point

"No exposure is created without passing the capital adequacy perimeter. The reservation locks bilateral exposure into the risk management system before any commercial commitment is made."

Step 3.3 — Convert

Go to Reservations. Click Convert to Order. Explain the frozen MarketplacePolicySnapshot.

4. Settlement Lifecycle

Core demonstration. Uses pre-seeded ESCROW_OPEN case.

Step 4.2 — Execution Sequence

1. Confirm Funds Final

Treasury confirms fiat receipt.

2. Allocate Gold

Vault ops confirms physical allocation.

3. Clear Verification

Compliance confirms KYC/KYB pass.

4. Authorize Settlement

Clearing Authority reviews & authorizes. Freezes capital snapshot.

5. Execute DvP

Atomic title/payment transfer. Status → SETTLED.

Key Talking Point

"This is the atomic settlement moment. Title transfer and payment transfer occur simultaneously in a single deterministic operation. There is no window where one party has delivered and the other has not."

Step 4.4 — Clearing Certificate

Click View Receipt on the settled order. Show the certificate number, SHA-256 hash, and ledger integrity statement.

Troubleshooting

Settlement actions not available?

Ensure you are logged in as Clearing Authority (Admin). Only admins can authorize and execute DvP.

Certificate missing?

The settlement must reach SETTLED status first. Complete all 5 lifecycle actions.

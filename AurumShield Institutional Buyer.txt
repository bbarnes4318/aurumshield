This is the most critical gate. The compliance engine runs four concurrent checks:

Gate	What It Does	Evidence

Technical flow:

Platform calls POST /api/compliance/cases/me/initiate
Compliance engine (compliance-engine.ts) evaluates counterparty readiness
KYCaid adapter creates a COMPANY applicant with hydrated affiliated_persons (UBOs and Authorized Signers)
Provider returns a redirect URL → buyer completes verification in KYCaid's hosted form
Webhook fires back → compliance case transitions: PENDING_USER → PENDING_PROVIDER → UNDER_REVIEW → APPROVED
UI auto-polls every 10 seconds and updates the live audit feed
IMPORTANT

All four gates must pass before the buyer can proceed. This is fail-closed architecture — any missing gate rejects advancement.

2. Allocated Custody — Not Pooled, Not Paper

Allocated = Specific, serialized bars are physically separated and titled to your entity. Bar serial numbers, assay certificates, refiner marks, and vault slot locations are on record.
Unallocated (what banks do) = You own a claim against a pool. If the custodian fails, you're an unsecured creditor. At $500M, you'd be in line behind everyone else.
Paper gold (ETFs like GLD) = You own shares in a trust. You have no right to specific bars. Redemption is at the trust's discretion. You cannot take physical delivery.
